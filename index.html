The Story

A Levantine pastry,
reimagined in chocolate.

Chocolush began at a home kitchen counter in 2023 — one small batch of pistachio-kunafa chocolate for a friend's birthday. The next morning, there were forty orders.

Today every bar is still tempered by hand, poured into a boutique mould, and finished with butter-crisped kataifi and whole Aleppo pistachios. We don't stock shelves. We ship what we made that morning.

The Craft

Four quiet steps.
Zero shortcuts.

A short ritual repeated, every morning, the same way. The recipe is forgettable. The discipline isn't.

01

Butter & Thread

Kataifi threads are pulled fine and toasted slowly in cultured butter until amber and audibly crisp.

02

Aleppo Pistachio

Whole kernels from the 2024 harvest are stone-milled into a loose praline — never a paste.

03

Tempered by Hand

Single-origin couverture is tabled on marble, brought into perfect crystalline temper, then poured.

04

Finished the Morning of

Bars rest overnight, are hand-wrapped at dawn, and leave the kitchen the morning they ship.
